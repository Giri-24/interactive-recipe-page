Chocolate Smoothie

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with sugar.
  4. Add the dry ingredients gradually to the egg mixture.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Preparation Time: 45 minutes | Servings: 4
